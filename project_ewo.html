The Philosophy of YouMatter Studio


When you look at gaming, you see a platform where numerous individuals enjoy spending most of their childhood.


However, how many of those children are actually represented in these games? If you look, the number of gamers who actually play versus the number of gamers who are actually represented is minimal. Realizing this, we wanted to create something that would allow everyone, in any shape or form be represented in something they love.


Thus, YouMatter Studio was launched.
